Some Quotes I Love

Little bits of wisdom

Posted by Clark on December 17, 2024

"To be loved but not known is comforting but superficial. To be known and not loved is our greatest fear. But to be fully known and truly loved is, well, a lot like being loved by God. It is what we need more than anything. It liberates us from pretense, humbles us out of our self-righteousness, and fortifies us for any difficulty life can throw at us."
- Timothy Keller, The Meaning of Marriage: Facing the Complexities of Commitment with the Wisdom of God

“Hatred does not cease by hatred, but by love alone.”
- Plaque at the memorial for the 2015 terrorist attacks on London Bridge

"True success in this life comes in consecrating our lives—that is, our time and choices—to God’s purposes (see John 17:1, 4; Doctrine and Covenants 19:19). In so doing, we permit Him to raise us to our highest destiny."
- Elder Christofferson

"Happiness in...life is most likely to be achieved when founded upon the teachings of the Lord Jesus Christ. Successful [living] is established and maintained on principles of faith, prayer, repentance, forgiveness, respect, love, compassion, work, and wholesome recreational activities."
- Me, from the Family Proclamation, inspired by Elder Rasband

"One’s life, therefore, cannot be both faith-filled and stress-free... Therefore, how can you and I really expect to glide naively through life, as if to say, 'Lord, give me experience, but not grief, not sorrow, not pain, not opposition, not betrayal, and certainly not to be forsaken. Keep from me, Lord, all those experiences which made Thee what Thou art! Then let me come and dwell with Thee and fully share Thy joy!'"
- Neal A. Maxwell

"The day is gone when you can be a quiet and comfortable Christian. Your religion is not just about showing up for church on Sunday. It is about showing up as a true disciple from Sunday morning through Saturday night—24/7! There is no such thing as a 'part-time' disciple of the Lord Jesus Christ. Jesus invited anyone who wants to be His disciple to take up His cross and follow Him. Are you ready to join the ranks?"
- Russell M. Nelson, “Disciples of Jesus Christ: Defenders of Marriage” (Brigham Young University commencement address, August 14, 2014)

"Men and women who turn their lives over to God will find out that he can make a lot more out of their lives than they can. He will deepen their joys, expand their vision, quicken their minds, strengthen their muscles, lift their spirits, multiply their blessings, increase their opportunities, comfort their souls, raise up friends, and pour out peace. Whoever will lose his life to God will find he has eternal life."
- "Jesus Christ—Gifts and Expectations," Ezra Taft Benson

"Moments of great calm, Kneeling before an altar Of wood in a stone church In summer, waiting for the God To speak; the air a staircase For silence; the sun’s light Ringing me, as though I acted A great rôle. And the audiences Still; all that close throng Of spirits waiting, as I, For the message."
- R.S. Thomas, Kneeling

“Will it make the boat go faster?”
- US Olympic rowing team

"God is anxiously waiting for the chance to answer your prayers and fulfill your dreams, just as He always has. But He can’t if you don’t pray, and He can’t if you don’t dream. In short, He can’t if you don’t believe."
- Elder Holland

"When we want to be something other than the thing God wants us to be, we must be wanting what, in fact, will not make us happy."
- C.S. Lewis

"If you have felt the influence of the Holy Ghost today, you may take it as evidence that the Atonement is working in your life."
- Elder Eyring

"The gift of the Holy Ghost adapts itself to all these organs or attributes. It quickens all the intellectual faculties, increases, enlarges, expands, and purifies all the natural passions and affections, and adapts them, by the gift of wisdom, to their natural use..."
- Parley P. Pratt, Key to the Science of Theology

To go places and do things that have never been done before – that’s what living is all about.
